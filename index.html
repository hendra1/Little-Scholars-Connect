A Phased MVP Strategy

Given the firm 9-month deadline and the request for a high-quality, "better UI/UX" application, a standard development approach is high-risk. A rushed project inevitably leads to poor user experience, bugs, and missed deadlines.

Therefore, our core strategy is to launch with a focused Minimum Viable Product (MVP). This strategy ensures we deliver the most critical features flawlessly for your school's opening. It prioritizes stability, usability, and the core-value propositions. More complex, secondary features will be developed and released in "Phase 2" immediately following the initial launch.

1. Design Sprint

Weeks 1-7

We validate the "Better UX" promise before writing a single line of code. High-fidelity Figma prototypes are mandatory.

2. MVP Core

Launch: Aug '26

Strict focus on operations: Attendance, Broadcasts, and Reports. Complex health logic is excluded to ensure stability.

3. Phase 2 Expansion

Finish: Nov '26

Post-launch, we build the "Vaccination Book" and "Growth Charts" features without deadline pressure, ensuring complex logic is handled correctly.

✨ AI Differentiation (Gen AI)

We use Gemini API to create "Magic Summaries" for teachers (converting keywords into full updates) and "Growth Insights" for parents (answering development questions), creating a unique selling point against competitors.

Feature Delivery Roadmap

Phase 1: Operational Essentials (Aug 2026)

The "Must-Have" features to ensure the school runs smoothly from Day 1.

  • Secure Family Portal Safe, role-based access ensuring parent data privacy and ease of login for busy teachers.
  • One-Tap Attendance Effortless check-in/out system with digital timestamps, giving parents peace of mind about their child's arrival.
  • Digital Bulletin Board A dedicated feed for newsletters and announcements, keeping the school community aligned without email clutter.
  • Direct Parent-Teacher Line Focused 1-to-1 text messaging for essential updates, keeping communication professional and organized.
  • Daily Moments Feed A chronological timeline of the child's day (meals, naps, play) with a daily photo highlight to connect parents to the classroom.
  • Growth Log Entry Simple digital recording of Height & Weight data, replacing paper logs for long-term tracking.

Phase 2: Gen AI & Health Suite (Nov 2026)

Advanced "Nice-to-Have" features powered by AI to differentiate the school.

  • ✨ Magic Daily Summaries Teachers enter quick keywords (e.g., "Nap, Rice, Happy"), Gen AI instantly drafts a warm, complete report for parents.
  • ✨ Growth Companion Parent AI Assistant: "Is my child's sleep normal?" answers based on WHO data & child's logs.
  • ✨ Smart Meal Planner Proactive dinner suggestions for parents based on the school lunch menu to ensure balanced daily nutrition.
  • Interactive Growth Charts Visual graphs plotting the child's height/weight against WHO standards, helping parents visualize development trends.
  • Vaccination Tracker Digital immunization record with automated reminders for upcoming shots, ensuring no health milestone is missed.
  • Smart Gallery Bulk photo uploads and tagged albums, making it easy for parents to find and save memories of their child.
