Heart-Centered UX

Balancing emotional connection with 1-tap efficiency.

Lean Strategy: "Design First, Code Second"

To fit the budget while hitting the "Better UI/UX" goal, we shift from a monthly retainer to a Fixed Milestone Model. We utilize BaaS (Firebase/Supabase) to minimize backend costs, allocating those savings to frontend polish.

1. Design Sprint

Weeks 1-7

We validate the "Better UX" promise before writing a single line of code. High-fidelity Figma prototypes are mandatory.

2. MVP Core

Launch: Aug '26

Strict focus on operations: Attendance, Broadcasts, and Reports. Complex health logic is excluded to ensure stability.

3. AI Differentiation

Phase 2: Sep '26

We use Gemini API to create "Magic Summaries" for teachers and "Growth Insights" for parents, differentiating us from competitors.

Why P2 Design is Critical

Phase 2 introduces complex interfaces like Growth Charts and AI Chat Interactions. These cannot be "improvised" by developers. A dedicated design sprint in August is required to define these flows before P2 development starts in September.

Feature Delivery Roadmap

Operational Essentials

Must-have features for the school to function on Day 1.

  • Secure Authentication Role-based login for Teachers & Parents.
  • Smart Attendance Check-in/out with timestamps.
  • Broadcast Feed School-wide announcements.
  • Basic Chat 1-to-1 Text messaging (No media).
  • Activity Summary Daily feed with text & single photo.
  • Basic Dev Tracking Manual Height/Weight Data Entry.

Gemini AI & Health Suite

Advanced features powered by Large Language Models to assist teachers and parents.

  • ✨ Magic Daily Summaries Teachers input keywords (e.g., "Nap, Rice, Happy"), Gemini generates a warm paragraph for parents.
  • ✨ Growth Companion Parent AI Assistant: "Is my 2yo's sleep normal?" answers based on WHO data & child's logs.
  • ✨ Smart Meal Planner Suggests dinner recipes based on what the child ate at school for lunch to ensure balanced nutrition.
  • Growth Analytics WHO standard visualization graphs.
  • Vaccination Book Digital records & due-date reminders.
