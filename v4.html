Value Vectors

Impact for every stakeholder.

For Teachers

Efficiency & Accuracy

By using AI to generate reports from uploaded photos, the system reduces administrative time by an estimated 40-60%, allowing educators to focus on student interaction rather than paperwork.

For Parents

Engagement & Reassurance

The "Instagram-style" gallery provides a familiar, warm UX. The AI Chatbot offers a 24/7 consultant for developmental questions, grounded in WHO standards.

For Institution

Data & Compliance

Builds a longitudinal dataset of student progress, enabling data-driven curriculum adjustments and ensuring compliance with national reporting standards.

Feature Breakdown

Core capabilities for MVP.

Teacher App

AI Photo Activity Analysis

Teachers simply upload photos of an activity (e.g., child stacking blocks). The AI analyzes the visual context and auto-drafts a developmental observation for review.

Automated Reporting

Voice-to-Text Notes

Dictate observations verbally ("Ian used his left hand...") and the app converts it to structured text.

Parent App

Smart Report Assistant

Parents can ask: "Show me Ian's progress report from last week" or access the Manual Menu to read curriculum details directly from trusted sources.

Grounded on Trusted Data

Moments Gallery

A private, secure feed of photos/images tagged with learning outcomes (Sensory, Motor, Cognitive).

Risk Analysis & Mitigation

Proactive strategies for project stability.

Risk Category Probability Impact Mitigation Strategy
Data Privacy (Legal) Medium High Strict adherence to PDP Law; Data localization in Jakarta; Mandatory parental consent flows; Encryption at rest/transit.
AI Hallucination High High RAG Architecture: Ground AI in WHO/CDC data. Human-in-the-Loop: Teachers must "Approve" AI drafts before they are sent to parents.
App Performance (Media) High Medium Implement optimized image loading/caching for varying Indonesian network speeds. Use FlashList for smooth UI performance on low-end devices.
Cost Overrun Medium Medium Use local object storage (Biznet Gio) for cold archiving. Implement strictly defined retention policies (e.g., delete raw media after 1 year).
